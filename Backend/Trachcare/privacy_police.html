TrachCare - Optimizing Tracheostomy Care

Abstract of Invention

Tracheostomy, a surgical procedure involving the creation of an airway through the neck, is often necessitated by various medical conditions. Following tracheostomy, patients require meticulous care to manage their airways, prevent complications, and optimize their quality of life. However, home care poses significant challenges due to its complexity and need for continuous monitoring.

TrachCare is a mobile application developed to address these issues by offering comprehensive tracheostomy care resources, including informative videos, emergency alerts, appointment reminders, and real-time support, ensuring both patients and caregivers can manage tracheostomy care effectively.

Features of TrachCare

  • Educational Resources: Videos on cleaning the inner tube, changing ties, and suctioning techniques.
  • Appointment Reminders: Notifications for follow-up appointments.
  • Tube Change Alerts: Timely alerts for tube replacements.
  • Emergency Guidance: Instructions during emergencies such as saturation drops, mucus plugs, or bleeding.
  • Communication with Healthcare Providers: Direct communication for advice and support.

Claims of the Invention

  • Education and Guidance: Step-by-step guides on tracheostomy care and emergency response protocols.
  • Care Coordination: Seamless communication between patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Prompt guidance on managing critical situations like saturation drops.
  • Wound Site Monitoring: Tracking wound healing with photo uploads for remote healthcare provider review.
  • Infection Prevention: Personalized recommendations for hand hygiene, equipment sterilization, and wound care.
  • Complication Monitoring: Advanced algorithms detecting early signs of airway obstruction or respiratory distress.
